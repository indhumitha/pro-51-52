ORIGAMI FACTS

  1. Origami is a japanesse art
  2. Origami is derived from two Japanese words - Ori (folded) and Kami (paper).
  3. Earlier, it was called Orikata (folded shape)
  4. Traditional origami uses a piece of paper in the shape of a square and does not permit any cutting.
  5. Largest origami crane measures 256 ft 6 inches.
  6. The record for the longest flight indoors of an origami plane is 22.48 seconds - held by Takuo Toda, who is credited with inventing the paper airplane.
